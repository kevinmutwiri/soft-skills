## An Introduction to Emotional Intelligence

When it comes to soft skills, **emotional intelligence** is a must for a young tech professional!

Now, it might be one of those concepts that you have heard of before and wondered, “What does that even mean?” So let’s talk about why it’s important. To do that, we want you to watch [this 3-minute video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=weuLejJdUu0), in which the 4 aspects of emotional intelligence are defined and shared.

As you watch make sure that you can answer these questions:

- What is emotional intelligence? Process of understanding and facilitating emotions into rational thinking
- What are the 4 simple aspects of emotional intelligence? perceiving emotions, understanding emotions, facilitating emotions (using emotions to facilitate thought), managing emotions

## The 4 Aspects of Emotional Intelligence

**What is emotional intelligence?** Emotional intelligence is _the process of understanding and facilitating emotions into rational thought_.

**Self-Awareness**
Using **emotions** to **facilitate** your **thoughts**: for example, knowing when you're feeling **anxious** or **nervous** so that you don't let it **affect your judgment** going into an interview.

**Self-Management**
Managing **your own emotions**: for example, the ability to **manage disappointment**, so that you can continue to make **progress** and **not be discouraged** by one failure.

**Social Awareness**
Understanding **others' emotions** and their associated **patterns**: for example, knowing when a **potential client** might be most likely to **accept an offer** you make.

**Relationship Management**
Ability to **perceive others' emotions** and **adjust accordingly**: for example, knowing if the other person is feeling **overwhelmed** so that you can adapt your **communication** accordingly.
