One of the biggest tools for your success is your mindset. It is important to know why and how you can change your thinking to achieve a healthy perspective and set of beliefs that will benefit you tremendously in your career and life.

There is also power in controlling your mind and emotions in response to situations. This is the key to making rational decisions that are in your best interests.

## Growth Mindset Recap

> Are your intelligence & abilities fixed at birth? Or can you improve and train them the way you train your muscles in the gym?

When people believe that they cannot change their intelligence through effort (a belief known as a “fixed mindset”) they have much poorer outcomes in many areas of life. However, when people understand that intelligence, talent, and skill can all be improved through effort, this is called a “growth mindset”. Many studies show that people with a growth mindset experience much more success than those with a fixed mindset.
