# Human Centered Design

> [home](./README.md)

Human-centered design is an approach to problem-solving that places deep empathy and understanding of people at its core.

Watch [this short video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=musmgKEPY2o) again to jog your memory on human-centered design. As you watch, ask yourself the following questions:

- What is the definition of human-centered design?
- What are the three phases of human-centered design? inspiration, ideation, implementation
