## Meditation: Try Something New

Ideally, you practice for at least 5 minutes, but 10 inhales and 10 exhales are WAY better than nothing!

You will feel calmer, happier, and more focused!

mindfulness meditation is simply the act of relaxing your body and focusing your attention on something (often your breath) to become more aware of the thoughts and feelings constantly moving through you.
