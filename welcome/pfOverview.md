## 1. How the program is structured.

Divided into 3 phases

### In Phase 1, You’ll:

* Be equipped with a foundational aptitude in the 8 Meta Skills.
* Be oriented to the current state of technology.
* Focus on the big challenges and opportunities currently found in our world.
* Gain a deeper awareness of your strengths and areas for growth.
> Reflect on where you feel most inspired and what you want to make an impact in

### In Phase 2, You’ll:

* Apply your newly acquired skills within a tech team setting.
* Work with a small group to address a big challenge or opportunity with the use of technology.
* Gain valuable experience that reflects common workspace configurations.
* Practice communicating and working with people with different personalities and interests.

### In Phase 3, You’ll:

* Explore the different technology tracks you can pursue.
* Learn about what teams, recruiting, and working in tech environments and industries look like.
* Hear from industry leaders, get hands-on experience in your chosen field, and gain practice with pitching.

## 2. How to approach the content.

### How to Succeed:

Set aside 30 to 40 hours a week for program activities and content.

Strategically manage your time.

Carefully review all material and instructions in Savanna.

Complete all exercises on time, especially quizzes and tests.

Complete all your milestones.

Engage with your local and online community when prompted.

Be an active, positive contributor to the program.

## 3. The 8 meta-skills covered in the program.

**Leading Self**: Developing self-awareness and setting personal career goals.

**Leading Others**: Improving collaboration, management, and leadership skills.

**Critical Thinking**: Analyzing complex problems and situations for key insights.

**Entrepreneurial Thinking**: Identifying opportunities for innovation and seizing them creatively.

**Quantitative Reasoning**: Using math, logic, and statistics to evaluate data and drive decisions.

**Communicating for Impact**: Effectively conveying messages to co-workers, clients, and managers.

**Managing Complex Tasks**: Using time management skills to plan and execute projects on time.

**Navigating Tech Ecosystems**: Building a personal tech toolkit and learning to adapt alongside the tech industry.

## 4. How your time in the program will be allocated.

**30 - 40 hrs a week for 12 weeks**

### Time Investment:

- 45% learning the content.
- 30% completing assessments and reflections.
- 15% interacting with your peers.
- 10% applying lessons learned within your local community.
