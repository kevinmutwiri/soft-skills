## **Requirements to Maintain Good Standing during the Course**

* **Learn** the course material every week and complete all activities and knowledge checks (MCQs at the end of each module).
* **Complete and submit** each week's test and milestone before the weekly deadline.
* If you miss a test or milestone **complete it** as soon as possible.
* If you fail a quiz or test, make sure you **attempt it again** immediately to get a passing score. *You have unlimited attempts until you reach the minimum passing score of 70%.*
* Ask for help from your community of peers and the **program team** during scheduled weekly **Office Hours.**
* Make the most of any **grace periods** that may be offered. Grace periods are a few days in which you can catch up on any missing/failed tests and milestones.
