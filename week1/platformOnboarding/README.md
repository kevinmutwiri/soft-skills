## Toolkit

Key tools, each serving a unique purpose to enhance your learning experience:

1. **Savanna** — Your _learning platform for accessing all course materials_.
2. **The eHub** — The _platform that contains all the Professional Foundations online learning community channels_ among other things. (basically all-in-one access point)
3. **LEA**— Our _Q&A platform where technical mentors and peers will assist you with course-related queries_.

All these tools will be accessible via single sign-on through the [eHub](), giving you everything you need right at your fingertips!

## Navigating the eHub

The eHub is your all-in-one access point to an exciting journey through the Professional Foundations Course and other ALX courses

It’s designed to make your experience seamless and efficient.
- Learning
- Community
- Rewards

## Savanna

**ALL course content** will be available here on **Savanna**. The course material is organized into **modules** with **projects**, similar to chapters, and new modules will be unlocked regularly, so you’ll always have fresh content to explore. 

You can check out the "**Projects**" section to see what you’ve completed and what’s still ahead. It’s best to go through the material in the order it’s presented!

## How to Submit a Milestone on Savanna

Complete quiz question (if present) so as to reveal milestone instructions

Go through milestone instructions

Complete each instruction

Upload file with completed milestone, Click submit. This just uploads file and does not submit it. You can click remove if you make a mistake and upload the wrong file.

Click on "submit" button to upload file. Click on "Check submission" and/or "Ready for review" buttons to submit the assessment
